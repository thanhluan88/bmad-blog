Phân tích Câu 791 — Bộ luyện PMBOK 8

A project manager is in the initiation phase of a new project and is preparing to develop a project team charter. The project team consists of members from different departments within the organization, and collaboration is vital for project success. What should the project manager do to create an effective project team charter?

Governance, ScheduleInitiatingManage Project ExecutionLead accountablyFull Bank Q791

1. PMBOK 8 mapping

Framework

Domain: Governance, Schedule
Focus Area: Initiating
Process: Manage Project Execution, Perform Integrated Change Control, Close Project or Phase
Principle: Lead accountably

2. So sánh đáp án

A — Sai Create the project team charter independently, outlining their expectations and goals for the team.

"Create the project team charter independently, outlining their expectations and go…" không phù hợp vấn đề trong đề bài — đáp án đúng tập trung "Hold a kickoff meeting with the team to collaboratively define the team's purpose,…".

B — Đúng Hold a kickoff meeting with the team to collaboratively define the team's purpose, roles, responsibilities, and goals.

Đáp án đúng theo PMBOK 8 và logic tình huống.

C — Sai Delegate the task of drafting the charter to a senior team member with experience in project management.

"Delegate the task of drafting the charter to a senior team member with experience …" không phù hợp vấn đề trong đề bài — đáp án đúng tập trung "Hold a kickoff meeting with the team to collaboratively define the team's purpose,…".

D — Sai Share a template of a generic project team charter with the team and invite them to modify it as needed to fit the project.

Tập trung "thông báo / truyền đạt thông tin" không giải quyết Share a template of a generic project team charter with the team and invite them to modify it as needed to fit the project. — đáp án đúng cần xử lý trực tiếp trọng tâm câu hỏi.

1. Câu hỏi thực hành — Full Bank Q277

pmp-full-questions · Câu 277
A project team adopted agile practices to meet the business need to develop an application. One of the senior team members inconsistently attends the team meetings, including the daily standup meetings. This senior team member then requests other team members to provide status updates on his/her behalf during the stand up meetings. What should the project manager do to handle this situation?

2. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: B

Signal trong stem Q277

Stem signals (ngữ cảnh Agile/Scrum) → B: Remind the senior team member to adhere to the team's working agreement.

→ B: Stem signals (ngữ cảnh Agile/Scrum) → B: Remind the senior team member to adhere to the team's working agreement.

Vì sao chọn đáp án này

  • B. Remind the senior team member to adhere to the team's working agreement. The project manager should remind the senior team member of the team's working agreement and ensure they stick to it. All other options are incorrect. Having other team members cover is also not sustainable and affects team dynamics. The project manager should first try to resolve this situation with the senior team member. Removal requests are a form of escalation and is done only if the Project Manager is unable to resolve the current issue.

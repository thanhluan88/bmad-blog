Stakeholders & engagement · 5

#1 Continuously identify and analyze stakeholders, not just at the beginnin…

Continuously identify and analyze stakeholders, not just at the beginning of the project.
VI: Identify/analyze SH liên tục cả đời dự án — không chỉ lúc kickoff.

#2 Actively engage all stakeholders, especially those who seem disengaged o…

Actively engage all stakeholders, especially those who seem disengaged or unhappy.
VI: Chủ động engage SH thờ ơ / không hài lòng — đừng bỏ qua.

#3 Engage individual stakeholders where appropriate, rather than always inv…

Engage individual stakeholders where appropriate, rather than always involving the entire group.
VI: Engage từng cá nhân khi cần — không bắt buộc họp cả nhóm mọi lúc.

#4 Document all impacted individuals as stakeholders, even if their involve…

Document all impacted individuals as stakeholders, even if their involvement is indirect.
VI: Ai bị impact cũng vào register — kể cả gián tiếp.

#5 Don't dismiss customer requests prematurely. Evaluate each one carefully…

Don't dismiss customer requests prematurely. Evaluate each one carefully.
VI: Đừng bác request khách sớm — evaluate kỹ trước khi từ chối.

Change & traditional planning · 4

#6 Traditional: Follow the plan. Do not allow changes without an approved c…

Traditional: Follow the plan. Do not allow changes without an approved change request.
VI: Predictive: bám plan — không đổi nếu chưa có CR được duyệt.

#7 Any stakeholder requesting changes to the project management plan must s…

Any stakeholder requesting changes to the project management plan must submit a change request.
VI: Muốn đổi PMP → phải submit change request.

#8 All changes must be reviewed and assessed.

All changes must be reviewed and assessed.
VI: Mọi change đều phải review/assess impact trước.

#9 Traditional: Never act without a plan. Planning is done once for the ent…

Traditional: Never act without a plan. Planning is done once for the entire project.
VI: Predictive: không hành động không plan — plan upfront cả dự án.

Investigate before act · 6

#10 Consult with your team and subject matter experts before making decision…

Consult with your team and subject matter experts before making decisions, especially on technical issues.
VI: Tham vấn team/SME trước khi quyết — đặc biệt kỹ thuật.

#11 Choose actions that best serve project objectives and deliver the highes…

Choose actions that best serve project objectives and deliver the highest value to the stakeholders.
VI: Chọn hành động tối ưu objective + value cho SH.

#12 Address issues directly and avoid unnecessary escalation.

Address issues directly and avoid unnecessary escalation.
VI: Xử lý issue trực tiếp — escalate chỉ khi cần.

#13 Anticipate and address potential problems before they become critical is…

Anticipate and address potential problems before they become critical issues.
VI: Anticipate sớm — đừng đợi thành critical.

#14 Always seek out the advice of the subject matter expert before moving fo…

Always seek out the advice of the subject matter expert before moving forward on a project. Never assume you are the expert.
VI: Hỏi SME trước khi tiến — đừng tự cho là expert.

#15 Always investigate and consult before acting, especially when the questi…

Always investigate and consult before acting, especially when the questions ask what the PM should do first or next.
VI: FIRST/NEXT → investigate/consult trước khi act.

People / conflict / servant leader · 12

#16 Address conflict directly and privately.

Address conflict directly and privately.
VI: Xung đột: xử lý trực tiếp + riêng tư.

#17 Always protect the team from external disruptions and distractions.

Always protect the team from external disruptions and distractions.
VI: Bảo vệ team khỏi nhiễu loạn bên ngoài.

#18 Be a servant leader.

Be a servant leader.
VI: Servant leadership: phục vụ / remove impediments / empower.

#19 Focus on the big picture, not just individual tasks or components.

Focus on the big picture, not just individual tasks or components.
VI: Nhìn big picture — không chỉ task lẻ.

#20 The team is best suited to break down work.

The team is best suited to break down work.
VI: Team tự break down work tốt nhất.

#21 Empower your team to make decisions and solve problems.

Empower your team to make decisions and solve problems.
VI: Empower team quyết định & solve problems.

#22 Foster a collaborative environment where team members feel comfortable a…

Foster a collaborative environment where team members feel comfortable asking for help and offering support.
VI: Môi trường cộng tác: hỏi giúp / hỗ trợ nhau thoải mái.

#23 Ensure team members are cross-trained and have diverse skill sets.

Ensure team members are cross-trained and have diverse skill sets.
VI: Cross-train / đa kỹ năng để giảm bottleneck.

#24 Provide timely and constructive feedback to your team members.

Provide timely and constructive feedback to your team members.
VI: Feedback kịp thời + mang tính xây dựng.

#25 Support and accommodate team requests for flexible work arrangements, if…

Support and accommodate team requests for flexible work arrangements, if feasible.
VI: Hỗ trợ linh hoạt giờ làm nếu khả thi.

#26 Use peer programming for skill development.

Use peer programming for skill development.
VI: Peer programming để phát triển kỹ năng.

#27 Address performance issues directly and privately, not in front of the e…

Address performance issues directly and privately, not in front of the entire team.
VI: Performance issue: riêng tư — không xử lý trước cả team.

Agile delivery & PO · 9

#28 The product owner documents and prioritizes features. Only the product o…

The product owner documents and prioritizes features. Only the product owner prioritizes the product backlog.
VI: Chỉ Product Owner ưu tiên product backlog.

#29 Agile teams love collocation (being together in one central place to wor…

Agile teams love collocation (being together in one central place to work).
VI: Agile thích collocation khi có thể.

#30 Face-to-face communication is the most effective way to communicate anyt…

Face-to-face communication is the most effective way to communicate anything.
VI: Face-to-face thường hiệu quả nhất (Agile Manifesto).

#31 Gather customer feedback regularly and throughout the project life cycle…

Gather customer feedback regularly and throughout the project life cycle.
VI: Thu feedback khách thường xuyên suốt vòng đời.

#32 Define quality requirements early and check them often.

Define quality requirements early and check them often.
VI: Quality requirements sớm + kiểm thường xuyên.

#33 Prioritize the highest value features in agile development.

Prioritize the highest value features in agile development.
VI: Agile: ưu tiên feature giá trị cao nhất trước.

#34 Focus on delivering working software frequently and iteratively.

Focus on delivering working software frequently and iteratively.
VI: Ship working software thường xuyên / iterative.

#35 The team should provide estimates for their own work.

The team should provide estimates for their own work.
VI: Team tự estimate việc của mình.

#43 In agile, the retrospectives are used to review and improve future sprin…

In agile, the retrospectives are used to review and improve future sprints or iterations.
VI: Retro: cải thiện sprint/iteration sau — không blame.

Risk / procurement / close / lessons · 6

#36 Actively identify and manage risks throughout the project.

Actively identify and manage risks throughout the project.
VI: Identify + manage risk chủ động suốt dự án.

#37 Continuously identify and assess risk throughout the life cycle of your …

Continuously identify and assess risk throughout the life cycle of your project.
VI: Risk cadence liên tục — không one-shot đầu dự án.

#38 Use mutually beneficial contracts and procurement.

Use mutually beneficial contracts and procurement.
VI: Hợp đồng / procurement win–win.

#39 Update the lesson learned register throughout the project.

Update the lesson learned register throughout the project.
VI: Lessons learned cập nhật suốt dự án — không chỉ lúc đóng.

#40 All projects should be formally closed, whether completed successfully, …

All projects should be formally closed, whether completed successfully, terminated, or terminated early, ensuring all bills are paid and resources released.
VI: Mọi dự án đều formal close (kể cả dừng sớm): bills + release resources.

#44 Implement feedback loops. Apply lessons from one task to the next.

Implement feedback loops. Apply lessons from one task to the next.
VI: Feedback loop: áp dụng lesson sang việc kế tiếp.

Vision & success criteria · 2

#41 Repeat and reinforce the vision to the team.

Repeat and reinforce the vision to the team.
VI: Lặp lại / củng cố vision với team.

#42 Clarify what success and failure look like.

Clarify what success and failure look like.
VI: Làm rõ success vs failure trông như thế nào.

Schedule (predictive vs agile) · 3

#45 Avoid cost and time overruns. If you must choose, fix budget issues befo…

Avoid cost and time overruns. If you must choose, fix budget issues before scheduling.
VI: Tránh overrun; nếu phải chọn — ưu tiên xử lý budget trước schedule (trong logic đề).

#46 Traditional: Understand the critical path.

Traditional: Understand the critical path.
VI: Predictive: nắm critical path.

#47 Agile: Manage schedule through sprint commitments and velocity.

Agile: Manage schedule through sprint commitments and velocity.
VI: Agile schedule: sprint commitments + velocity.

Exam answer craft · 3

#48 When you see answers that are absolute, like "always" or "all," they're …

When you see answers that are absolute, like "always" or "all," they're probably not correct.
VI: Đáp án tuyệt đối (always/all) — thường sai.

#49 Never do nothing.

Never do nothing.
VI: Đừng chọn “không làm gì”.

#50 The perfect answer isn't always listed. You must choose from the choices…

The perfect answer isn't always listed. You must choose from the choices available.
VI: Không có đáp án hoàn hảo — chọn best available.

Luyện 10 câu (retrieval · interleaved)

Unhappy SH

Backlog

Conflict

Absolute option

SME

Servant leader
Servant leadership trên đề thường nghĩa?
Closeout
Dự án bị hủy giữa chừng. PM?
Do nothing
Option “wait and see / do nothing”?

Cheat sheet

IF stem asks FIRST / NEXT → investigate / consult (#15) IF unhappy / silent stakeholder → actively engage (#2) IF predictive scope/baseline change → CR · review · assess (#6–#8) IF agile product backlog priority → Product Owner only (#28) IF interpersonal / performance conflict → direct + private (#16/#27) IF option says always / all / never → usually wrong (#48) IF option is do nothing / wait indefinitely → discard (#49) IF project ends early → still formal close (#40) IF technical unknown → SME / team first (#10/#14)

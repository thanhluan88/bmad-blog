Bài học Q50 về Benchmarking — HR Group cần cải thiện customer satisfaction nhanh; team không tìm được cách improve product; sponsor muốn proven ideas từ similar projects or organizations.

PMBOK Guide 8, p.41 & p.149 Elicit and Analyze Requirements Câu 50 — Practice

1. Benchmarking (PMBOK p.149)

Benchmarking compares actual or planned products, processes, and practices with those of comparable organizations to identify good practices and improvement opportunities.

Benchmarking

May involve internal or external projects across different industries — provides proven ideas and a basis for measuring performance. Không chỉ estimate cost/time.

  • Compare với similar projects / organizations
  • Identify good practices đã proven elsewhere
  • Improvement opportunities cho product/process

2. Benchmarking vs các đáp án khác

TechniquePurposeQ50 fit?
Benchmarking Compare with similar orgs — proven ideas, good practices ✓ (A)
Analogous estimating Estimate duration/cost từ similar past projects Estimating — không identify improvement ideas (B)
Focus groups Collect stakeholder perspectives (Q43) Không compare với external similar orgs (C)
Review with senior developer Internal technical review Không systematic external comparison (D)

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 50

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q50
A project has been launched at HR Group to improve customer satisfaction within a short timeframe. During planning, the team struggles to identify ways to improve the product. The Project Sponsor would like proven ideas based on what has worked well in similar projects or organizations. What should the project manager do next?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: A

Tại sao chọn A?

  • Similar projects/organizations → benchmarking compares comparable orgs (p.149).
  • Proven ideas / good practices → identify what worked elsewhere.
  • Struggles to improve product → external comparison, not just internal review.

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng
B. Analogous estimating Estimate cost/duration from similar projects — không elicit improvement ideas. “Analogous” trong stem gây nhiễu!
C. Focus groups Stakeholder input (Q43) — không compare với similar organizations.
D. Senior developer review Internal only — sponsor muốn proven ideas từ bên ngoài/similar orgs.

Liên hệ Q43 vs Q50

Q43: differing expectations → focus groups and benchmarking (gather). Q50: proven ideas from similar organizations → benchmarking alone (A). Đừng nhầm “analogous” trong câu hỏi với analogous estimating.

5. Drill — Match technique to scenario

Proven ideas from similar projects/organizations
Estimate project duration from past similar project
Differing stakeholder expectations on features
Identify good practices from comparable orgs

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: “Similar projects” → Analogous estimating (B)

Analogous = estimate time/cost. Stem cần proven improvement ideas — benchmarking.

Bẫy 2: Stakeholders → Focus groups (C)

Focus groups collect internal stakeholder views — không compare external similar orgs.

Bẫy 3: Improve product → Senior developer (D)

Internal review — sponsor explicitly wants what worked in similar organizations.

Pattern

Similar projects/organizations · proven ideas · good practices · compare → Benchmarking (A).

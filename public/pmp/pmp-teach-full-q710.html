The project manager must ensure deliverables are completed on time, within budget, and to the required quality standards. The sponsor wants the project to start immediately with all essential project artifacts in place. What should the project manager do first?

1. Đáp án tham chiếu (Reference solution)

  • D. Develop a detailed work breakdown structure (WBS) to define scope and organize the project’s activities.
  • The WBS establishes the scope baseline, essential for aligning deliverables with cost and schedule.

  • Scenario: A sponsor has met with key stakeholders to launch a high-profile project.
  • PMBOK 8, p. 71 (Plan Stakeholder Engagement): Plan Stakeholder Engagement is the process of developing strategies to engage identified project stakeholders based on their needs, expectations, interests, requirements, and poten… — so the PM must address the relationship directly with transparency before escalating or assigning blame.
  • Therefore D is correct: The WBS establishes the scope baseline, essential for aligning deliverables with cost and schedule.

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

D. Develop a detailed work breakdown structure (WBS) to define scope and organize the project’s activities. The WBS establishes the scope baseline, essential for aligning deliverables with cost and schedule. With baselines set, the WBS ensures structured execution readiness. The other options are incorrect. The stakeholder register helps engagement, but without the WBS, deliverables cannot be structured or tracked effectively. The quality plan ensures standards but depends first on clear deliverable definitions in the WBS. The integrated project management plan is finalized after subsidiary elements like the WBS are prepared.

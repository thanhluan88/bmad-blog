Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

C. Work with the product owner to break down backlog stories further and update the definition of ready before pulling items into sprints. Working with the product owner to further break down stories and update the definition of ready (DoR) ensures that only well-elaborated, actionable stories are pulled into sprints. This directly addresses the root cause of the recurring incomplete story pattern. The other answer choices are incorrect. Reducing sprint velocity targets is incorrect because it treats the incomplete stories symptom without addressing the root cause, which is insufficient story elaboration. Fewer stories that are still poorly defined will produce the same outcome. Conducting a retrospective is incorrect because it does not address the structural issue of stories not meeting readiness criteria before they enter a sprint. The product owner's involvement in story refinement is the key intervention. Implement a story review checkpoint at sprint planning is incorrect because it is a detection mechanism, not a prevention mechanism.

Vì sao chọn đáp án này

  • A, E A. Independent, negotiable, and estimable E. Valuable, small, and testable These options align with INVEST principles. INVEST is an acronym that is commonly considered to stand for the following characteristics: independent, negotiable, valuable, estimable, small, and testable. INVEST helps to ensure that user stories are focused on delivering value to the customer, which can lead to higher customer satisfaction and retention. This consistency breaks down user stories into smaller, more manageable pieces, and the team can more accurately estimate the time and effort required to complete each task. By focusing on smaller, more manageable tasks, the team can reduce the risk of delays and bottlenecks that can occur when working on larger, more complex stories. Finally, using the INVEST model promotes transparency and accountability within the team.

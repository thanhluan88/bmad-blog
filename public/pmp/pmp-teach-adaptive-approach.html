Practice Questions — PMBOK 8th Ed

Bài học Q15 về Adaptive Approaches — agile/change-driven khi requirements volatile; short iterations, feedback, và một người chịu trách nhiệm scope/value.

The Standard for PM, p.63 Development Approach Câu 15 — Practice

1. Adaptive Approach (PMBOK 8)

Adaptive approaches (change-driven / agile) phù hợp khi requirements và technical solution highly uncertain, volatile, likely to change. Vision rõ sớm; requirements được elaborate dần theo feedback.

Định nghĩa chính thức (PMBOK p.63)

A clear vision is established early, and known requirements are progressively elaborated, changed, or replaced based on stakeholder feedback, the environment, or unexpected events.

Sprint 1 Sprint 2 Sprint 3 Sprint n

Short iterations · deliver features regularly · refine from feedback

Product Owner — one person responsible for approved scope & value to the organization

Keywords trong đề thi → Adaptive

  • Short iterations / sprints
  • Deliver features regularly
  • Refine based on stakeholder feedback
  • Mobile app, digital products, high uncertainty
  • Product Owner owns scope & value

2. Adaptive vs Predictive (Q14 ↔ Q15)

Q15 — Adaptive (A) ✓ Iterations, feedback, evolving requirements, Product Owner
Q14 — Predictive (D) Phase gates, defined early, strict change control, waterfall
ApproachKhi nàoĐặc điểm
Adaptive (A) Uncertain, volatile requirements Iterations, feedback, progressive elaboration
Predictive (B) Scope stable early Upfront planning, phase gates
Hybrid (C) Mix stable + evolving Not described in Q15 scenario
Incremental (D) Deliver portions over time May overlap agile but Q15 keywords = full adaptive pattern

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 15

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q15
Jason is leading a mobile application project at Blue Peak Digital Solutions. The team works in short iterations, delivering features regularly and refining their approach based on feedback. One person is responsible for the approved scope and its value to the organization. Which development approach is being used?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: A

Tại sao chọn A?

  • Short iterations + regular feature delivery + feedback = agile/adaptive hallmark.
  • One person for scope & value = Product Owner role (slide annotation).
  • Mobile app at digital solutions firm = typical adaptive context (volatile requirements).
  • Khớp PMBOK p.63 — change-driven / agile approaches.

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không phải
B. Predictive Waterfall/plan-driven — trái iterations và evolving requirements (Q14 pattern).
C. Hybrid Scenario mô tả thuần adaptive, không nhắc phần predictive.
D. Incremental Deliver từng phần có thể overlap nhưng đề nhấn iterations + feedback + PO = adaptive (A) đầy đủ hơn.

Synonyms Adaptive (p.63)

change-driven · agile · progressive elaboration · stakeholder feedback · volatile requirements · Product Owner

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Chọn D — Incremental vì “deliver features regularly”

Incremental = usable portions over time. Khi có short iterations + feedback + PO, PMBOK 8 gọi là Adaptive — đáp án đầy đủ hơn.

Bẫy 2: Nhầm với Q14 Predictive

Q14 = regulated, defined early. Q15 = mobile app, iterations, feedback — đối lập rõ ràng.

Pair study Q14 + Q15

Đề thi thường đặt predictive và adaptive cạnh nhau — đọc keywords, không đoán theo ngành.

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

B. Update the stakeholder engagement plan to include specific hybrid touchpoints for operations stakeholders. In a hybrid environment, stakeholder engagement must be deliberately designed across both agile and predictive elements. Updating the stakeholder engagement plan allows the project manager to define appropriate mechanisms (e.g., sprint reviews, demos, joint working sessions, hybrid ceremonies) and frequencies that fit operations stakeholders’ needs while preserving agile team focus. The other options are incorrect. Daily coordination meetings are meant for the agile team, and adding many external stakeholders can reduce their effectiveness. It also responds tactically without addressing the broader engagement strategy in a hybrid context. Scheduling additional phase‑gate reviews reinforces only the predictive side and does not leverage agile’s iterative feedback opportunities. It increases formality, time, and bureaucracy without improving early collaboration within the hybrid model. Assigning a business analyst to summarize agile outputs may help with communication, but it still treats operations stakeholders as passive recipients instead of active participants. It is a partial workaround, not a systematic adjustment to the stakeholder engagement approach.

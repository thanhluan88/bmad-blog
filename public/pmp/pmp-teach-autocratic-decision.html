Practice Questions — PMBOK 8th Ed

Bài học Q39 về Autocratic Decision-Making — critical update phải implement ngay để tránh system failure; team divided, time limited; Marina cần act quickly.

PMBOK Guide 8, p.29 & p.163 Assess and Implement Changes Câu 39 — Practice

1. Autocratic Decision-Making

Khi time is critical và cần quick decision, PM có thể dùng autocratic decision-making — act decisively mà không đợi consensus từ team đang divided.

Autocratic decision-making (PMBOK p.163)

Appropriate when time is critical and a quick decision is required, allowing the project manager to act decisively without waiting for consensus.

2. Chọn style theo tình huống

StyleKhi nàoQ39?
Autocratic (D) Time critical · prevent failure · team divided ✓ Act quickly
Group facilitation (A) Có thời gian build consensus Team already divided + no time
Majority voting (B) Democratic — vẫn cần process time Too slow for emergency
Multi-criteria analysis (C) Thorough evaluation of options Too analytical when immediate action needed

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 39

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q39
Marina is managing a power grid stabilization project at Grid Core Energy. A critical update must be implemented immediately to prevent system failure, but the team is divided on the best approach and time is limited. Marina needs to act quickly to move forward. What should Marina do next?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: D

Tại sao chọn D?

  • Emergency: prevent system failure → time critical.
  • Team divided — consensus sẽ mất thời gian.
  • PM acts decisively — autocratic (p.29, p.163).

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng NEXT
A. Group facilitation Cần thời gian consensus — team divided + emergency.
B. Majority voting Voting process — không đủ nhanh cho critical update.
C. Multi-criteria analysis Thorough analysis — quá chậm khi must implement immediately.

Không phải lúc nào cũng autocratic

PMP thiên về collaborative leadership — nhưng khi đề có immediately, prevent failure, time limited + divided team → autocratic là đúng.

5. Drill — Style nào?

Critical fix now — team divided, time limited
Complex trade-off — time available for team input
Compare multiple options with weighted criteria
Quick decision required — no time for consensus

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Team divided → Facilitate group (A)

Collaboration tốt thường ngày — nhưng emergency + divided = autocratic.

Bẫy 2: Multiple options → MCDA (C)

MCDA thorough — không phù hợp implement immediately prevent failure.

Bẫy 3: "PM shouldn't dictate" → reject D

PMP favors servant leadership — except when time critical (explicit signals).

Pattern

Immediately + prevent failure + time limited + team divided → Autocratic decision-making (D).

An organization is running two similar projects simultaneously. The sponsor emphasizes the importance of avoiding repeated mistakes and maximizing efficiency across both initiatives. The project manager needs to ensure that lessons identified in one project are applied systematically to the other through a knowledge transfer mechanism. What should the project manager do?

The project manager should first conduct information-sharing sessions to capture tacit knowledge and lessons learned formally in the lessons learned register and distribute them across both active project teams.

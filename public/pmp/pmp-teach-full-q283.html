Practice Questions — PMBOK 8th Ed · Q283

Bài học Q283 về Manage Stakeholder Engagement (PMBOK 8, tr. 72) — Stakeholder không hài lòng

A project is halfway complete with good schedule performance, but the customer has identified several minor issues requiring clarification. Direct communication between the customer and development team would resolve these quickly, but team members express concerns about engaging the customer directly. Some worry about technical explanations, others about scope creep from informal discussions, and a few lack confidence in customer-facing communication. The project manager wants to enable direct engagement while maintaining project control. What should be the primary approach?

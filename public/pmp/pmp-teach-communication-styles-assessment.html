Practice Questions — PMBOK 8th Ed

Bài học Q77 về Communication Styles Assessment — Isabelle tại Zenith Trust Bank, digital banking rollout: teams cần product info và training nhưng stakeholders avoid updates và minimal feedback.

PMBOK Guide 8, p.73 & 155 Plan Communications Management Communication Styles Assessment Câu 77 — Practice

1. Communication styles assessment (p.155)

Technique evaluates preferred communication methods, formats, and content for stakeholders. Often used with unsupportive stakeholders who avoid updates. May follow stakeholder engagement assessment to identify gaps and determine need for tailored communication activities.

Methods Email, chat, in-person, video
Formats Brief, visual, detailed, formal
Content Product info, training, updates

Instructor: Avoid updates + minimal feedback

More meetings (A) or formal reports (D) won't fix mismatch — assess how they prefer to receive information first → communication styles assessment (C).

2. So sánh các đáp án

OptionActionQ77 fit?
A. More status meetings Increase meeting frequency They avoid updates — more meetings likely worsen problem
B. Engagement plan Update engagement strategies Broader plan — stem needs comms style diagnosis first
C. Styles assessment Evaluate preferred methods/formats/content Yes — tailor comms to resistant stakeholders
D. Formal reports Send reports to receiving team One-size-fits-all — they already give minimal feedback

Q76 vs Q77

Q76: structure comms (management plan). Q77: stakeholders resist current approach → assess preferred styles before tailoring activities.

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 77

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q77
Isabelle is planning the rollout of a digital banking project at Zenith Trust Bank. Multiple teams are receiving the benefit of the rollout and will need product information and training, but stakeholders in those areas frequently avoid updates and provide minimal feedback. What should Isabelle do?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: C

Tại sao chọn C?

  • Avoid updates + minimal feedback = unsupportive communication pattern.
  • Styles assessment finds preferred methods, formats, content (p.155).
  • Enables tailored comms activities — may follow engagement gap analysis (Q75).

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng
A. More meetings Stakeholders avoid updates — pushing more meetings ignores their preferences.
B. Engagement plan Plan-level update — need diagnostic tool for communication preferences first.
D. Formal reports Assumes formal written format works — they already provide minimal feedback.

5. Drill — Technique nào?

Stakeholders avoid email updates — discover preferred channels
Map current vs desired engagement levels across all stakeholders
Team requests weekly standup — already engaged and collaborative
Compliance requires monthly audit report to regulator

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Low feedback → more meetings (A)

They avoid updates — increasing meetings is the opposite of tailoring to their style.

Bẫy 2: Stakeholder issue → engagement plan (B)

Engagement plan is broader — styles assessment diagnoses how to communicate effectively.

Bẫy 3: Need info → formal reports (D)

Product info needed — but delivery method must match preferences; assess styles first.

Pattern

Avoid updates · minimal feedback · unsupportive stakeholders → communication styles assessment (C).

C — Conduct a communication styles assessment.

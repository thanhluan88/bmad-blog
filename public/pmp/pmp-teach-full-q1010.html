Đáp án

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

A. Meet with the sponsor and key stakeholders to discuss the impact of the new law and identify a response strategy. Meeting with the sponsor and key stakeholders to discuss the law's impact and identify a response strategy is the correct proactive approach. It ensures decision-makers are informed and that a plan is developed before the law affects the project. The other answer choices are incorrect. Pausing planning while waiting for the law to be enacted is incorrect because it delays the project unnecessarily. The team should proactively assess the potential impact and plan a response based on available information. Update the law as a risk and assigning a monitor is incorrect because it is a passive response that does not engage the right stakeholders in understanding the impact or developing a strategy. Engaging a legal consultant is incorrect because it should follow, not precede, stakeholder alignment on the impact and the need for legal guidance. Leadership must first be engaged before external experts are brought in.

In a high-visibility global project, the virtual team spans multiple time zones and includes stakeholders with varying priorities and cultural norms. Recently, several stakeholders have stopped attending meetings, some complain about scheduling, and others ignore invitations, causing delays in decision-making and misalignment on key deliverables. What should the project manager do?

Chi tiết tiếng Anh — Why this answer

1. Đáp án tham chiếu (Reference solution)

  • B. PM sửa kế hoạch / baseline / schedule để xử lý trực tiếp trọng tâm câu hỏi — phù hợp miền Stakeholders + Governance + Scope, Focus Area Initiating (PMBOK 8).

2. Lý luận PMBOK 8 (PMBOK reasoning)

  • Scenario: A client's company announces a reorganization, which includes changes in the senior management involved in the project.
  • PMBOK 8, p. 151 (Performance Domain: Stakeholders, Manage Stakeholder Engagement): In an environment where management activities are centralized, accountability (being answerable for an outcome) is usually assigned to one — so the PM must address the relationship directly with transparency before escalating or assigning blame.
  • Therefore B is correct: 71: Manage Stakeholder Engagement — aligns with this scenario.

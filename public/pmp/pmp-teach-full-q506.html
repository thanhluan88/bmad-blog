Phân tích Câu 506 — Bộ luyện PMBOK 8

A pharmaceutical project team has gathered quality requirements from stakeholders. These include regulatory standards, patient safety expectations, and internal performance benchmarks. The project manager needs to ensure that these requirements, along with overall project expectations, are met systematically. What should the project manager do in this scenario?

1. PMBOK 8 mapping

Framework

Domain: Governance, Scope
Focus Area: Executing
Process: Manage Project Execution, Perform Integrated Change Control, Close Project or Phase
Principle: Adopt a holistic view, Embed quality

2. So sánh đáp án

A — Sai Begin conducting quality inspections and statistical sampling of the selected deliverables to verify compliance with the identified quality standards.

"Begin conducting quality inspections and statistical sampling of the sel…" cùng nhóm đảm bảo tuân thủ / compliance nhưng lệch trọng tâm — "Define quality standards and select appropriate quality tools and techni…" sát vấn đề chất lượng / defect hơn.

B — Đúng Define quality standards and select appropriate quality tools and techniques for assurance and control, for integration into the project management plan.

Đáp án đúng theo PMBOK 8 và logic tình huống.

C — Sai Perform a quality audit to confirm that the team’s processes already align with the organizational quality standards and regulatory expectations.

"Perform a quality audit to confirm that the team’s processes already ali…" cùng nhóm đảm bảo tuân thủ / compliance nhưng lệch trọng tâm — "Define quality standards and select appropriate quality tools and techni…" sát vấn đề chất lượng / defect hơn.

D — Sai Escalate unresolved differences in quality standards and expectations to the sponsor and document them in the issue log for executive resolution.

Ghi nhận lessons learned / cập nhật log là quan trọng nhưng thường là bước sau khi đã xử lý tình huống — không phải hành động ưu tiên khi câu hỏi hỏi FIRST/NEXT. (Câu hỏi hỏi ACTION — đây không phải bước ưu tiên.)

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

B, C B. Change the status of the "Specify design elements" to "In Progress". C. Change the assigned individuals for the "Specify design elements" row to "Stakeholders". The CEO has requested a change to one of the original design elements, indicating that, even though the item was previously marked as "Completed," it must be revisited to incorporate the required modification. Reflecting its progress accurately as "In Progress" ensures that the new activity for approval is tracked appropriately in the matrix. Since the CEO has emphasized that approval of the change must involve all stakeholders, the responsibility assigned to meet this requirement should align with this directive. Changing it from "Team Lead" to "Stakeholders" aligns with the newly indicated involvement.

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

A. Review and update the requirements as an input to re-baselining the scope and reprioritizing the combined product backlog. Reviewing and updating the requirements as an input to re-baselining the scope and reprioritizing the backlog is the correct approach. It ensures the combined program has a coherent, accurate scope baseline and a prioritized backlog that reflects the new shared objectives. The other answer choices are incorrect. Submitting a change request without first updating the requirements to reflect the merged scope is incorrect because it risks formalizing an inaccurate or conflicting baseline. Freezing all development until a new charter is approved is incorrect because it is overly conservative and may cause unnecessary delays. Maintaining separate scope baselines and backlogs is incorrect because it defeats the purpose of combining the projects and perpetuates the conflicts and overlaps that prompted the merger. A unified scope and backlog are necessary for effective hybrid program management.

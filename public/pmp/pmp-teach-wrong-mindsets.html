1. Risk vs Issue — “chưa chắc” thì chưa đổi plan

could / potential / full impact not yet known → RISK REGISTER + MONITOR
Đúng (Q948): Funding delay có thể ảnh hưởng budget/timeline nhưng impact chưa rõ → log risk and monitor.
Trap: Pause toàn bộ · Escalate sponsor ngay · Revise budget/schedule ngay · CR trước khi shortage “chắc”.
Q975: Timber shortage potential → giảm dependency (multi-vendor), không change request sớm.

Liên quan glossary: susceptible (risk driver), force majeure ≠ contingency plan.

2. Authority — swarm không tạo thêm quyền

outside team’s authority / cannot resolve at PM level → ESCALATE
1. DetectBlocker / delay
2. AskTrong quyền team?
3a. YesSwarm / RCA / fix
3b. NoEscalate sponsor
Q1080: Critical task delay + actions outside authority → escalate sponsor (không swarm session).
Q1020: New law, legal implication beyond PM → escalate + issue log + change process.
Q1014 nuance: Compliance may apply, chưa rõ → research + recommend cho sponsor — không pause charter, không nhét assumption mù.

3. Compliance — hệ thống liên tục, không giấy một lần

regs change mid-project → CONTINUOUS MONITOR + EXPERTS + AUDIT/TRACK
  • Q959: Gaps vs updated standards → continuous regulatory monitoring.
  • Q964: Safeguard noncompliance → schedule an audit.
  • Q1024: Future regs → tracking system cho backlog alignment.
  • Q1044: Ethics → OPA tạo governance framework (integrity/transparency).
  • Q1047: Local vs HQ → legal validate + escalation procedures — không authorize ethics exceptions.
  • Q1083: Design + safety protocols → đưa team OPA + charter trước khi hire/benchmark.

4. Change control — đi tiếp đúng process

informal changes already happened → ENFORCE CCB GOING FORWARD (ít khi reverse all)
Q978: Scope đổi không qua CCB → đảm bảo thay đổi tương lai đi formal process.
CCB already approved → VERIFY IMPLEMENTATION (quality audit)
Q996: Không chỉ update change log/baseline — run quality audits xem change đã implement hiệu quả.

5. Lessons learned phải actionable

lessons / failed project → INTEGRATE INTO PLANS + RISK + SHARE (no blame)
TrapĐúng
Chỉ archive / xem video / interviewIntegrate vào upcoming project plans (Q942)
Blame who was wrong / generic docsRisk discussions + share for performance (Q1006 A,C)
Workshop cuối bắt buộcDùng sớm cho planning & skill

6. Value với sponsor trước khi nhận scope

sponsor cites new tech / market share → ASSESS VALUE → UPDATE PLAN
Q1061: Không báo delay ngay / không team tự cap scope — làm việc với sponsor đánh giá value rồi mới revise strategy.
Q1060: Success metrics = safety incident rate + weather delay days + SPI — không vanity counts.
Bonus agile (Q1113): PO đổi giữa sprint → discuss + assess impact — không ignore, không “agile nên nhận hết và vẫn on time”.

Cheat sheet

PMP WRONG-SET MINDSET (luannt115 · full · wrong>0)

1 RISK vs ISSUE
   potential / could / impact unknown → log risk + monitor
   ✗ pause all  ✗ escalate first  ✗ revise baseline early

2 AUTHORITY
   outside authority / beyond PM → escalate (+ issue/CR if needed)
   ✗ swarm  ✗ slip schedule alone

3 COMPLIANCE CONTINUUM
   regs update → continuous monitor + experts
   safeguard → audit | future regs → tracking
   ethics → OPA governance | local vs HQ → legal + escalation
   ✗ one-shot templates  ✗ authorize exceptions

4 CHANGE FORWARD
   informal past → enforce CCB going forward
   CCB approved → quality audit effectiveness
   ✗ reverse everything first

5 LESSONS → ACTION
   integrate plans | feed risk | share for performance
   ✗ blame  ✗ archive-only  ✗ generic docs

6 VALUE BEFORE SCOPE
   sponsor idea/tech → assess value → update plan
   metrics → SPI + what sponsor prioritized
   PO mid-sprint change → discuss impact (Q1113)

PMBOK Guide 8, p.23–24 & p.151 Manage Quality Assurance Câu 32 — Practice

1. Checklists trong Manage Quality Assurance

Checklist là danh sách items hoặc actions dùng làm reminders — thường phát triển từ historical information và lessons learned từ dự án tương tự. Giúp team thực hiện tasks nhất quán, giảm unnecessary work và defects.

Checklists (PMBOK p.151)

A checklist is a list of items or actions used as reminders, developed from historical information and lessons learned from similar projects to identify potential risks.

Work with the team để tạo checklists — collaboration đảm bảo alignment và cải thiện performance mà không cần escalation hoặc major restructuring.

2. Đọc tín hiệu đề

Senior: inefficiencies in delivery Process không tối ưu, không aligned
Defects occurring Hậu quả của inconsistent execution
Missing deadlines Performance gap — cần practical tool, không restructure

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 32

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q32
A project team is missing deadlines due to inconsistent task execution. Junior members are completing extra, unnecessary work, while senior members report inefficiencies in the way items are delivered, with defects occurring as a result. What should the project manager do?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: C

Tại sao chọn C?

  • Inconsistent execution + unnecessary work + defects → checklist standardizes steps.
  • Collaborate with team — alignment without escalation or restructuring.
  • Checklists từ lessons learned — practical QA tool (p.151, Manage QA p.23–24).

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng
A. Swarm Agile technique cho blocker cụ thể — không address systemic inconsistent execution.
B. General training Quá broad — đề cần targeted alignment tool, không generic program.
D. Reassign to experienced Major restructuring / workaround — không fix root cause cho cả team.

Liên hệ Q30 & Q31

Q30: process improvement cho shift inconsistency. Q31: audits cho process gaps. Q32: checklists cho inconsistent task execution at team level — lighter-touch, collaborative fix.

5. Drill — Tool nào phù hợp?

Inconsistent steps causing defects across junior & senior
Single urgent blocker needs all hands now
Team lacks fundamental skill in new technology
Reminders from lessons learned on similar projects

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Problem → Swarm (A)

Swarm giải quyết blocker tức thì — không standardize task execution cho cả team.

Bẫy 2: Inefficiency → General training (B)

"General" quá rộng — đề hỏi alignment & consistency, checklist cụ thể hơn.

Bẫy 3: Senior frustrated → Reassign (D)

Workaround, không scalable — không giúp junior stop unnecessary work.

Pattern

Inconsistent execution + defects + no escalation asked → Work with team on checklists (C).

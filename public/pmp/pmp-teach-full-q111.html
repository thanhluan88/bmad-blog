Tại sao chọn D?

  • D is correct (reference solution): D. Create a project charter at the beginning of the project, including identified team stakeholders. A project charter is the recommended tool to set appropriate stakeholder expectations. It is usually created to set stakeholder expectations at the beginning of the project.

Đáp án

D. Create a project charter at the beginning of the project, including identified team stakeholders.

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

D. Create a project charter at the beginning of the project, including identified team stakeholders. A project charter is the recommended tool to set appropriate stakeholder expectations. It is usually created to set stakeholder expectations at the beginning of the project. The other options are incorrect. Listing scope exclusions does not keep stakeholders actively involved or establish a shared understanding throughout the project. Providing continuous communication and integrating stakeholder feedback into status reports are necessary tasks for ensuring credibility, but these are ongoing requirements that help manage expectations rather than setting them initially. While developing a risk register with stakeholders to mitigate potential issues, it addresses risk documentation, but it does not engage stakeholders in establishing or managing their expectations. True stakeholder involvement requires proactive collaboration and alignment from the start.

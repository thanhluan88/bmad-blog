As the transformation initiative progresses, different business units interpret strategic objectives and success measures differently, leading to variations in local prioritization, funding decisions, and delivery tradeoffs. Senior leadership worries that, without intervention, these divergent interpretations will fragment execution and weaken overall value realization, and asks how to restore alignment on vision without suppressing necessary local context. What should the project manager do?

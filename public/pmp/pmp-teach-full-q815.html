1. Câu hỏi thực hành — Full Bank Q815

pmp-full-questions · Câu 815
A project is nearing completion, and the customer proposes developing and publishing a business case to highlight the benefits delivered. The project manager and team are concerned that, at this stage, sufficient data may not be available to fully validate the benefits, and premature publication could misrepresent the project’s actual value. What should the project manager do?

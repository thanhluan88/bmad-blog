Bài học Q48 về Brainstorming — Jane, BLS customer portal: team collaborate tốt nhưng chỉ có handful of ideas; sponsor muốn speed things up khi gather requirements.

PMBOK Guide 8, p.41 & 149 Elicit and Analyze Requirements Brainstorming Câu 48 — Practice

1. Brainstorming (p.149)

Kỹ thuật group để generate ideas quickly, thường do facilitator dẫn. Bao gồm idea generation và analysis — gather data, solutions, ideas từ stakeholders, SMEs, và team members trong Elicit and Analyze Requirements (p.41).

Khi nào dùng brainstorming?

Cần more ideas fast — team đang work well together, chỉ thiếu volume of ideas. Open, rapid ideation session.

2. Brainstorming vs các kỹ thuật khác

TechniqueBest forQ48
Brainstorming (B) Generate many ideas quickly in group Handful of ideas + speed up
Affinity diagram (A) Organize/group ideas after generation After brainstorm — không tạo ideas nhanh
Interviews (C) Deep 1-on-1 input, sensitive topics Slower — sequential, không speed up group ideation
NGT (D) Equal participation when loud voices dominate Q47 problem — team already works well together

Liên hệ Q47 vs Q48

Q47: loud voices dominate → NGT (structured, equal). Q48: few ideas, need speed → brainstorming (rapid group ideation).

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 48

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q48
Jane is gathering requirements for a customer portal at BLS. The team are working well together but have only generated a handful of ideas so far. The Project Sponsor would like to speed things up. What should Jane do next?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: B

Tại sao chọn B?

  • Need more ideas + sponsor wants speed → facilitated group brainstorming.
  • Team collaborates well — good fit for open ideation session.
  • Elicit and Analyze Requirements — brainstorming (p.41, 149).

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng
A. Affinity diagram Sort/group existing ideas — không generate volume quickly.
C. Interviews 1-on-1 sequential — slower than group brainstorm for rapid ideation.
D. NGT Structured equal participation — Q47 when dominance is problem, not few ideas.

5. Drill — Brainstorm, affinity, interview hay NGT?

Few ideas — sponsor wants to speed up ideation
Many sticky notes — group into categories
Loud voices dominate — need equal participation
Sensitive confidential input from one executive

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Ideas → Affinity diagram (A)

Affinity organizes ideas — stem cần generate MORE ideas fast, không sort.

Bẫy 2: Users → Interviews (C)

Interviews chậm hơn group brainstorm — sponsor wants speed.

Bẫy 3: Group technique → NGT (D)

NGT = Q47 dominance problem. Q48: team works well, chỉ cần more ideas.

Pattern

Handful of ideas · speed things up · team working well → Brainstorming (B).

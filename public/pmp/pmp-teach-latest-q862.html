1. Câu hỏi thực hành — Exam Latest Q862

pmp-exam-latest · Câu 862 · Chọn nhiều
A project manager was recently assigned to a banking IT project. The project team has been developing products using a predictive approach for more than 10 years, but management wants the team to change to an agile approach. The project manager met with the team to introduce the agile approach. Most of the team members complained about changing approaches because the performance of this team has always been above average among the IT teams in the bank. Which two actions should the project manager take? (Choose two) agile.

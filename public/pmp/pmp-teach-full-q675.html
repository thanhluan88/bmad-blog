Đáp án

B. Conduct regular procurement performance reviews with all vendors to ensure that products and services are delivered on time and in accordance with the contract.

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

B. Conduct regular procurement performance reviews with all vendors to ensure that products and services are delivered on time and in accordance with the contract. By conducting procurement performance reviews on a regular basis, the project manager can proactively identify and address any potential issues with vendor performance. This will help ensure that the project meets its procurement objectives and that the products are delivered on time and meet the quality requirements. The other options are incorrect. Updating the risk register is important in general, but it does not directly respond to the quality and delivery time issue. Delegating the work to procurement will ensure that vendors abide by the contract terms, but this is not enough to guarantee that the products meet the quality requirements and are delivered on time. Reviewing the vendor management plan will not be enough to identify and address any potential problems as the work progresses. It is essential to ensure alignment with vendors, but this should be part of a comprehensive process for monitoring their performance.

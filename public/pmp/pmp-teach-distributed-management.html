1. Lead the Team & Distributed Management (p.85, 168)

Distributed management (p.168) là approach phân quyền decision-making, cho phép team tự tổ chức (self-organize) và empower các thành viên để quyết định thay vì chờ leader. Thuộc Lead the Team domain (p.85) — leadership approach cho team hiện đại, đặc biệt trong bối cảnh toàn cầu (global teams) hoặc remote.

Autocratic Leader quyết định mọi thứ; team thực hiện. Dùng khi cần quyết định nhanh hoặc high-risk.
Distributed Leader empower team; các thành viên tự quyết định. Cải thiện ownership, speed, innovation.
Servant Leadership Leader hỗ trợ team từ phía sau; focus vào remove barriers. Khi team cần coaching.
Standup / Reporting Daily sync; không thay đổi quyền hạn decision-making. Chỉ là coordination tool.

Signal trong stem

Team resolved conflicts · improved skills · building trust · BUT waiting for her direction → team READY cho distributed management (empower + decentralize), không phải servant leadership (B) hay thêm stand-ups (C) hay autocratic (A).

ApproachMục đíchQ90?
Distributed (B) Empower team tự quyết định — decentralize decision-making ✓ Team ready; break bottleneck; build ownership
Autocratic (A) Leader quyết định tất cả ✗ Create bottleneck; không leverage team capability
Increase stand-ups (C) Daily sync meetings ✗ Coordination — không fix decision-making bottleneck
Servant Leadership (D) Leader remove barriers; coach team ✗ Team không cần coaching; cần empower + authority

2. So sánh các đáp án & Q39 Autocratic vs Q88 Servant

OptionActionQ90 fit?
A. Autocratic decision-making Leader quyết định mọi thứ; team thực hiện lệnh Create bottleneck — team chờ, không grow ownership (instructor: X)
B. Apply distributed management Empower team tự quyết định; decentralize authority Yes — break bottleneck; leverage team readiness
C. Increase stand-ups/reporting More daily meetings; more visibility Coordination tool — không fix decision bottleneck (instructor: X)
D. Apply servant leadership Leader support from behind; remove obstacles Team không cần coaching — cần authority to decide (instructor: X)

Q39 Autocratic vs Q90 Distributed vs Q88 Servant Leadership

Q39 Autocratic: dùng khi team inexperienced, high-risk decision, cần nhanh (centralize control).
Q90 Distributed: dùng khi team ready, skilled, trust built — empower họ quyết định (decentralize control).
Q88 Servant: dùng khi team cần coaching, remove barriers, leader support từ phía sau. Ba cái khác biệt: autocratic = tight control; distributed = empower; servant = support/coach.

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 90

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q90
Louise is a project manager at SG Tech, leading a globally distributed software integration team. The team has resolved conflicts with each other, improved their technical skills, and built strong mutual trust. However, the team is waiting for her direction on most decisions, which is slowing progress and creating bottlenecks. What should Louise do next?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: B

Tại sao chọn B?

  • Team ready: resolved conflicts, improved skills, strong trust — không cần autocratic hoặc servant support.
  • Bottleneck: waiting for Louise's direction → cần empower họ để quyết định tự do (distributed management, p.168).
  • Distributed = decentralize decision-making, self-organize, ownership — break dependency on leader.
  • Lead the Team domain (p.85) — leadership approach thích hợp khi team mature.

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng NEXT
A. Centralized control Tạo bottleneck lớn hơn — team chờ lệnh, không grow. Team ready để empower.
C. Increase stand-ups More meetings ≠ fix decision authority. Bottleneck = lack of autonomy, không lack of sync.
D. Servant leadership Coaching + remove barriers là tuyệt vời, nhưng team không cần — cần empower + decision rights.

So với Q39 Autocratic & Q88 Servant

Q39: team inexperienced → autocratic (centralize).
Q88: team need coach → servant leadership (support from behind).
Q90: team mature & ready → distributed (empower + decentralize). Bối cảnh khác nhau, leader approach khác.

5. Drill — Autocratic, Distributed, Servant, hay Standup?

Team resolved conflicts, built trust, but waiting for manager direction → slowing progress
Inexperienced team, critical decision, high risk → need quick leader decision
Team skilled but facing external blockers; leader removes obstacles and coaches
Mature team, high trust, self-organize — empower to decide without leader approval
Daily team coordination; sync on progress, blockers, dependencies

0 / 5 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Mature team → keep control (A)

Tempting to stay autocratic khi team ready — thực ra team cần empower để grow ownership và speed.

Bẫy 2: Bottleneck → more meetings (C)

Stand-ups help coordination nhưng không fix decision authority. Bottleneck = waiting for approval, không lack of sync.

Bẫy 3: "Support" keyword → servant leadership (D)

Servant leadership tuyệt vời nhưng Q90 context = team ready để tự quyết, không cần coaching/barrier removal từ leader.

Bẫy 4: "Global team" confuses với servant

Global = diverse perspectives, need empower + distributed, không servant coaching. Q88 = Q88, Q90 = Q90.

Pattern

Team ready (resolved conflict, built trust) · waiting for direction · bottleneck → Apply distributed management (B).

7. Flashcard

Nhấn để lật

Distributed management vs Autocratic?
Distributed: empower team tự quyết, decentralize authority (team ready, mature). Autocratic: leader quyết mọi thứ (team inexperienced, high-risk).
Q90 đáp án?
B — Apply distributed management and empower the team to make decisions.
Q39 vs Q88 vs Q90?
Q39: autocratic (team inexperienced). Q88: servant (team need coach). Q90: distributed (team ready, empower).

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

A. Facilitate a team discussion to evaluate the sprint goal and definition of done (DoD) and identify the most effective approach. In adaptive environments, the project manager facilitates collaborative decision-making. By focusing the discussion on the sprint goal and definition of done, the project manager helps the team evaluate options and reach consensus. This approach manages conflict constructively and supports team ownership. The other options are incorrect. Making a unilateral decision reduces team engagement and may ignore valuable insights. It conflicts with the self-organizing nature of adaptive teams. Postponing a critical technical decision can introduce uncertainty into sprint planning and jeopardize the sprint goal. Adaptive teams favor timely decisions to maintain flow. Escalating to functional managers undermines the team’s autonomy and can delay resolution. Adaptive teams should resolve most conflicts within the team, with facilitation from the project manager or agile leader.

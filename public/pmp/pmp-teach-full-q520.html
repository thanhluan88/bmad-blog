Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

C. Consult with the product owner to identify the minimum viable product needed for launch and reprioritize the release plan accordingly. Consulting with the product owner to identify the MVP and reprioritize the release plan is the correct agile response. It focuses delivery on the features that provide the most urgent business value, enabling an earlier release that satisfies the stakeholders' core needs. The other answer choices are incorrect. Escalating to the sponsor before engaging the product owner is incorrect because it bypasses the agile value chain. Revising the schedule without engaging the product owner is incorrect because it risks compressing the wrong work. Prioritization must precede schedule changes. Conducting a full stakeholder workshop to redefine scope is incorrect because it is a intervention that takes more time than the situation warrants.

A data center hardware manufacturer is designing a new component for enterprise environments. During a planning session, the project sponsor demands that the already tight schedule be shortened by two months to align with a new product launch. The engineering lead warns that critical path tasks cannot be reduced without cutting scope. The client insists that all features must remain in the final version. What should the project manager do?

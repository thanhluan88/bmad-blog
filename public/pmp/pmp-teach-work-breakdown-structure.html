Practice Questions — PMBOK 8th Ed

Bài học Q51 về Work Breakdown Structure (WBS) — RLM product development: organize deliverables, decompose the work into manageable components để support planning and execution.

PMBOK Guide 8, p.41–42 Define Scope Câu 51 — Practice

1. Define Scope & WBS (PMBOK p.41–42)

Define Scope develops a detailed or high-level description of the project, product, and value to be delivered. Trong predictive approaches, deliverables được structured trong WBS.

Work Breakdown Structure (WBS)

Hierarchical decomposition of total project work into manageable components — organizes deliverables, supports planning & execution. Output của Define Scope (predictive).

WBS level (concept)Content
Level 1Project
Level 2Major deliverables / phases
Level 3+Work packages — manageable components

2. Predictive vs adaptive — decompose how?

ApproachOrganize workQ51 fit?
Predictive — WBS Organize deliverables · decompose for planning/execution ✓ (A)
Adaptive — user stories Capture requirements iteratively in backlog Agile artifact — không WBS decompose (B)
Adaptive — product backlog Prioritize features for incremental delivery Delivery prioritization — không organize all deliverables (C)
Validate deliverables Confirm completed work with stakeholders Control/acceptance — sau execution, không decompose (D)

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 51

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q51
Jonah is managing a product development project at RLM. The team needs to clearly organize all deliverables and decompose the work into manageable components to support planning and execution. What should Jonah do next?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: A

Tại sao chọn A?

  • Organize deliverables + decompose work → WBS (Define Scope, predictive).
  • Manageable components for planning & execution → hierarchical WBS decomposition.
  • PMBOK: predictive approaches structure deliverables in WBS (p.41–42).

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng
B. User stories Adaptive/agile elicitation — không organize all deliverables into WBS.
C. Product backlog Prioritize incremental delivery — agile, không decompose to planning components.
D. Validate deliverables Acceptance/validation sau khi complete — không phải decompose step.

Liên hệ Q44 vs Q51

Q44: hybrid streams merged → RTM track requirements (before/alongside scope). Q51: organize & decompose deliverables → WBS (Define Scope, predictive).

5. Drill — WBS, backlog hay validate?

Decompose deliverables for planning & execution
Iterative requirements in adaptive approach
Confirm completed deliverables with stakeholders
Predictive — structure all deliverables hierarchically

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Product development → User stories (B)

Product dev không luôn agile — stem nói organize deliverables & decompose → WBS predictive.

Bẫy 2: Features → Backlog (C)

Backlog = prioritize delivery — không decompose all deliverables for planning.

Bẫy 3: Deliverables → Validate (D)

Validate = sau khi complete — stem hỏi next step để plan/execute, không accept.

Pattern

Organize deliverables · decompose work · planning and execution → WBS (A).

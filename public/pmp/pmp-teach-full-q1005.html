Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

A. Include a risk impact matrix and a risk register with status updates in project communication reports. When communicating risk impact to stakeholders, the project manager should provide the risk register to capture outputs from the risk management processes, an impact matrix to visually represent risk probability and severity, and risk status in project reports, including a heat map, the consequences of potential risks, and the actions planned to mitigate or manage them. The other options are incorrect because they do not directly address communication with stakeholders or the severity of risk impact. The project manager does not need to use rolling wave planning because the risk impact is ongoing and is not limited to a short-term versus long-term focus. A contingency reserve is time or money assigned in the schedule or cost baseline for known risks, regardless of their impact. The schedule performance index is a measure of schedule efficiency expressed as the fraction of earned value to planned value.

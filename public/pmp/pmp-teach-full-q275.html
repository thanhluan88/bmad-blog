Chi tiết tiếng Anh — Why this answer

1. Đáp án tham chiếu (Reference solution)

  • B. Create a responsibility assignment matrix (RAM) to identify which team member is responsible for each task.
  • RAM is used to define the roles and responsibilities of stakeholders, including team members within a project.

2. Lý luận PMBOK 8 (PMBOK reasoning)

  • Scenario: Team members assigned to a complex project are having trouble understanding their specific duties and their roles within the project.
  • PMBOK 8, p. 47 (Performance Domain: Schedule, Develop Schedule): Revising and maintaining the project schedule to sustain a realistic schedule should continue throughout the project. — which is why this PMBOK guidance applies directly to the stem and supports the correct PM action.
  • Therefore B is correct: RAM is used to define the roles and responsibilities of stakeholders, including team members within a project.

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

B. Create a responsibility assignment matrix (RAM) to identify which team member is responsible for each task. RAM is used to define the roles and responsibilities of stakeholders, including team members within a project. This action clarifies roles and responsibilities across the project team ensuring accountability, reducing confusion, and improving coordination. The other options are incorrect. None of these tools or techniques are used to define the roles and responsibilities of team members. A high-level project management plan is a strategic plan used to outline a project's objectives, deliverables, scope, and timelines. A probability-consequence matrix is used in risk management to plot the probability of a risk event occurring against the consequences if it does occur. It helps in prioritizing risks and determining appropriate responses. SMART guidelines help ensure that project objectives are well-defined and trackable.

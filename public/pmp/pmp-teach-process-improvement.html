Practice Questions — PMBOK 8th Ed

Bài học Q30 về Process Analysis & Improvement — defects lặp lại do inconsistent procedures across shifts; cần xử lý root cause, không chỉ ghi nhận defect hay cập nhật instructions.

PMBOK Guide 8, p.23 & p.198 Manage Quality Assurance Câu 30 — Practice

1. Manage Quality Assurance & Process Improvement

Manage Quality Assurance thực hiện các hoạt động có hệ thống từ quality management plan để đảm bảo outputs đáp ứng requirements. Process improvement phân tích và nâng cao processes — giảm waste, tăng consistency và customer satisfaction.

Process improvement (PMBOK p.198)

Process improvement focuses on analyzing and enhancing processes to increase efficiency, quality, and value by identifying improvements, reducing waste, and achieving better performance and customer satisfaction.

Tools gắn với root cause analysis: 5 Whys, Ishikawa (fishbone).

2. Root cause vs triệu chứng

Signal trong đềÝ nghĩa
Repeatedly identify defects Pattern — không phải one-off defect
Inconsistent procedures across shifts Process problem — root cause
Address underlying cause Process analysis & improvement (D)
Rework and delays Hậu quả — fix process, không chỉ count defects

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 30

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q30
Omar is managing a pharmaceutical packaging project at NCL. Quality inspections repeatedly identify defects caused by inconsistent procedures across shifts, leading to rework and delays. Omar wants to address the underlying cause and improve consistency moving forward. What should Omar do next?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: D

Tại sao chọn D?

  • Underlying cause = inconsistent procedures → analyze & improve process.
  • Manage Quality Assurance + process improvement (p.23, p.198).
  • Repeated defects across shifts = systemic process issue, not just documentation tweak.

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng NEXT
A. Update work instructions Có thể là output sau improvement — chưa analyze root cause trước.
B. Check sheets Tool ghi nhận/count defects — không fix inconsistent procedures.
C. Assign quality lead Organizational fix — không address process analysis trực tiếp.

Liên hệ Q11 Embed Quality

Q11: embed quality vào processes. Q30: khi processes inconsistent gây defects lặp lại → process analysis & improvement.

5. Drill — Tool/action nào?

Inconsistent procedures causing repeated defects
Tally defect types during inspection
5 Whys / Ishikawa on shift variance
Need dedicated QA ownership long-term

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Defects → Check sheets (B)

Check sheet đếm/record — không analyze và improve inconsistent procedures.

Bẫy 2: Inconsistency → Update instructions (A)

Cập nhật WI là symptom fix — cần process analysis trước để hiểu root cause across shifts.

Pattern

Underlying cause + repeated process defects → Process analysis & improvement (D).

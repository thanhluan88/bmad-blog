One option produces visible results that boost short-term confidence while deferring risks; the other addresses risks and lowers costs long-term but is less noticeable. Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

D. Suggest a delivery approach that balances immediate progress with risk reduction and operating cost savings over time. Proposing a delivery path that deliberately balances short-term visible progress with long-term risk reduction and operating cost impact frames the recommendation around value, explicitly balancing visible progress and long-term risk and cost implications, aligning delivery choices with sustained outcomes. The other answer choices are incorrect. Choosing the path with the most visible outputs optimizes short-term optics at the expense of addressing known risks and long-term value. Selecting the path that focuses only on reducing long-term operating costs prioritizes long-term efficiency without considering the need to maintain stakeholder support and momentum. Alternating paths avoids a clear value-based choice and fails to communicate or manage explicit tradeoffs.

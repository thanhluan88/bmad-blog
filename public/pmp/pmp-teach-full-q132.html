Signal trong stem Q132

→ C: explain agile value (CI + feedback) trước ceremony / escalate / lecture EQ.

Tại sao chọn C?

  • PM facilitate thảo luận để team/stakeholder cùng giải quyết — Build an empowered culture.
  • Manage Project Execution + Lead accountably — đáp án C align PMBOK 8.
  • Tham chiếu PMBOK 8, tr. 21.

Đáp án

C. Schedule brainstorming sessions to foster a collaborative, trust-based environment and make the team’s tacit knowledge more accessible.

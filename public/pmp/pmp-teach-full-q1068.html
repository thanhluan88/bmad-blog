Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

B. Apply cultural and political awareness tools to evaluate decision‑making influences. Applying cultural and political awareness tools is the right interpretive step after data collection and before strategy. The other options are incorrect. Updating stakeholder maps is incorrect because stakeholder maps show relationships, not cultural or political dynamics for decision-making directly. Reviewing summaries is incorrect because doing so without analysis is passive and abdicates responsibility.

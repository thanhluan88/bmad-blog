Bài học Q83 về Responsibility Assignment Matrix (RAM) — Michelle tại VEE smart grid: team mới hình thành, xung đột ai responsible cho task nào, miss deliverable đầu tiên.

PMBOK Guide 8, p.81 & 194 Plan Resource Management RAM / RACI Câu 83 — Practice

1. RAM — Responsibility Assignment Matrix (p.194)

RAM gán resources cho work packages, liên kết activities với team members. Làm rõ roles, responsibilities, và authority — đảm bảo một người accountable cho mỗi task. RACI là dạng RAM phổ biến.

RResponsible — làm việc
AAccountable — chịu trách nhiệm cuối
CConsulted — tư vấn
IInformed — được thông báo

Signal trong stem

"Performing tasks others feel they are responsible for" = role overlap / confusion → workshop RAM (A), không phải RBS hay Tuckman review.

ArtifactMục đíchQ83?
RAM / RACI Ai responsible/accountable cho task nào ✓ Role confusion
RBS Phân cấp categories of resources ✗ Không clarify task ownership
Team Charter (Q82) Values, agreements, operating guidelines ✗ Blame culture — khác role overlap

2. So sánh các đáp án

OptionActionQ83 fit?
A. Workshop RAM Clarify R/A/C/I per activity Yes — fix responsibility overlap
B. Workshop RBS Resource breakdown hierarchy Wrong artifact — instructor: X (instructor: RBS tree)
C. Update schedule Review project schedule Symptom (missed date) — root cause is role confusion
D. Tuckman Model Review forming/storming stages Awareness only — doesn't assign who does what

Q82 vs Q83

Q82: blame + rework → team charter. Q83: overlapping responsibilities + missed deliverable → RAM/RACI.

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 83

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q83
Michelle is planning a smart grid project at VEE, where the team have recently formed. Over the last two weeks several conflicts have arisen, with some team members performing tasks that other team members feel they are responsible for. The team have just missed their first deliverable due date. What should Michelle do next?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: A

Tại sao chọn A?

  • Conflict = ai responsible cho task nào không rõ → RAM (p.194).
  • RAM links activities ↔ team members; one accountable per task.
  • Workshop với team — collaborative RACI build.
  • Missed deliverable là hậu quả — fix root cause (roles) trước schedule tweak.

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng
B. RBS Resource Breakdown Structure = hierarchy of resource types — không assign task ownership.
C. Schedule Schedule không resolve role overlap — có thể update sau khi RAM clear.
D. Tuckman Forming/Storming/Norming/Performing/Adjourning — giáo dục team stage, không gán R/A/C/I.

5. Drill — RAM, RBS, Schedule, hay Tuckman?

Two members both think they own the same integration task
Break down project resources by skill category and team
Critical path slipped — rebaseline dates after scope confirmed
No single accountable owner per work package — deliverables slipping

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Resource → RBS (B)

RAM và RBS đều thuộc Plan Resource Management — nhưng RBS là breakdown hierarchy, không clarify task responsibility.

Bẫy 2: Missed date → schedule (C)

Missed deliverable là symptom. Stem nhấn "responsible for" → fix roles trước.

Bẫy 3: New team + conflict → Tuckman (D)

Storming stage giải thích dynamics — nhưng không assign ai làm gì. RAM workshop mới fix overlap.

Pattern

Role overlap · "responsible for" · task confusion → Workshop RAM (A).

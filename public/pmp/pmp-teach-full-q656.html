1. Câu hỏi thực hành — Full Bank Q656

pmp-full-questions · Câu 656
An internal auditor finds several gaps in the company's adherence to quality processes. The project sponsor argues the gaps are minor and should not delay progress. The quality assurance manager insists that corrective actions must be implemented before continuing. The project management office warns that ignoring these findings could harm compliance certifications. What should the project manager do to ensure compliance?

Đáp án

D. Implement corrective actions immediately to ensure adherence to quality standards before resuming project work.

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

D. Implement corrective actions immediately to ensure adherence to quality standards before resuming project work. The project manager should perform corrective action immediately when quality issues are detected. Implementing corrective actions immediately maintains project integrity, reduces rework, and supports consistent delivery of high-quality outcomes. The other options are incorrect. Ignoring gaps is not ethical and risks certification failure. Postponing corrective actions increases the chance of hidden defects. It is important that stakeholders are informed, but simply discussing compliance with stakeholders does not proactively address the issue at hand.

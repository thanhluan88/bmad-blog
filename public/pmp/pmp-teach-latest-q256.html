1. Đáp án tham chiếu (Reference solution)

  • C. PM chủ động engagement stakeholder: làm rõ kỳ vọng, nhu cầu thông tin và thống nhất hướng xử lý.

2. Lý luận PMBOK 8 (PMBOK reasoning)

  • Scenario: A project manager is working on a multinational project that has more than 100 stakeholders.
  • PMBOK 8, p. 76 (Performance Domain: Stakeholders, Manage Stakeholder Engagement): – Stakeholder engagement assessment matrix • Communication skills – Feedback – Presentations • Interpersonal and team skills – Active listening – Cultural awareness – Leadership – … — so the PM must address the relationship directly with transparency before escalating or assigning blame.
  • Therefore C is correct: 71: Manage Stakeholder Engagement — aligns with this scenario.

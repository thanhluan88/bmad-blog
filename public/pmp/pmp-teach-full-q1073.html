1. Câu hỏi thực hành — Full Bank Q1073

pmp-full-questions · Câu 1073
A startup tech company has hired a project manager to lead a project designing a new piece of hardware. The manager brings valuable experience from a more established company, but at this company, uncertainty and change are common. Some of the manager’s conventional practices are meeting resistance from the team. Although willing to make adjustments, the project manager is focused on the project's success. What should the project manager do?

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

B. Re-evaluate expectations about hierarchy to trust the team members’ knowledge and empower them with decision-making. An organization’s culture, including its tolerance for uncertainty and change, is at the root of how it operates. The project manager will have better results by replacing the hierarchical expectations of the previous company with the more egalitarian one that works for this startup. The other options are incorrect. Planning a rigid schedule would not ensure that the design process followed it. The company’s culture is partly a response to the conditions under which it operates, and a design project especially is subject to unpredictability. Having already received some resistance from the team, the project manager should realize that trying to import a more formal leadership style from the previous company was a mistake. The project manager should put effort into determining what style works best for this particular team. Although establishing a code of conduct for meetings might be a good idea, that would depend on whether such an explicit statement was needed. An already well-functioning team might perceive the attempt as superfluous and potentially insulting. The need for documenting policies and procedures would depend on organizational requirements rather than on the project manager’s preferences for them.

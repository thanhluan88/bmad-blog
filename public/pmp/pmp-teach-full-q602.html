1. Câu hỏi thực hành — Full Bank Q602

A national retail chain has initiated a rebranding project. During an executive review of the project, stakeholders note that milestones are on track and costs are within baseline. The project sponsor warns that low-value features are being released before critical ones. Other stakeholders argue that the schedule was approved and should not change. What should the project manager do?

Tại sao chọn B?

  • B is correct (reference solution): B. Reassess backlog priorities with stakeholders to focus on features delivering the highest business value. The project manager should facilitate reprioritization to align with business value.

Đáp án

The project sponsor meets with the client to showcase a prototype. During the demonstration, the project sponsor promises to deliver two months earlier than the baseline. Upon being informed of this promise, the engineering team advises this commitment is unrealistic. The project management office stresses that formal governance must approve any change in schedule. What should the project manager do?

Answer

D. Explain to the sponsor and client that acceleration requires an impact analysis and change request approval.

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

D. Explain to the sponsor and client that acceleration requires an impact analysis and change request approval. The project manager should perform an impact analysis and seek change request approval. Explaining that acceleration necessitates an impact analysis and change request approval promotes informed decision-making, maintains governance, and prevents unmanaged risks or cost overruns. The other options are less effective. Blindly committing to the revised schedule without additional resources risks failure. Inflating the budget because of an over-commitment leads to cost overruns. Committing to the sponsor's promise without an impact analysis poses the risk of cost and time overruns.

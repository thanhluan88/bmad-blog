Bài học Q228 về Identify Stakeholders (PMBOK 8, tr. 200) — Stakeholder/phòng ban mới

An engineering firm is working on a government contract to deliver a highly complex project. Midway through project execution, a key stakeholder is replaced by a new government official with oversight authority. At the next governance review, the new stakeholder criticizes the project team for failing to engage the stakeholder in recent project decisions. What should the project manager do first?

Grounding PMBOK 8

PMBOK 8 · Identify Stakeholders + Lead accountably, tr. 200

Đáp án đúng — C

C. Update the stakeholder register and conduct tailored onboarding to integrate the stakeholder into project communications and decision-making.

Stakeholder mới xuất hiện giữa chừng Executing — PM phải perform stakeholder analysis, xác định nhu cầu thông tin/báo cáo, rồi update communications management plan cho phù hợp.

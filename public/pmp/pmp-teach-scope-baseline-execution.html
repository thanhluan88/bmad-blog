Practice Questions — PMBOK 8th Ed

Bài học Q28 về Manage Project Execution — stakeholder lo ngại consistency và reliability của outputs; PM đảm bảo deliverables theo scope baseline.

PMBOK Guide 8, p.22–23 Manage Project Execution Câu 28 — Practice

1. Manage Project Execution

Process này gồm lead và perform work theo project management plan, implement approved changes, và đảm bảo deliverables đáp ứng scope requirements.

Quality focus tại execution (p.22–23)

At this point, quality should be focused on ensuring that deliverables are being produced according to the agreed-upon requirements set in the scope baseline.

2. Scope Baseline gồm gì?

Scope Statement
WBS
WBS Dictionary
Work Packages

Khi stakeholder hỏi về consistency/reliability của outputs — PM xác nhận team đang build đúng agreed-upon requirements, không mở lại planning hay escalate ngay.

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 28

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q28
You are managing a production system upgrade at Iron Works Manufacturing. During execution, your team is delivering outputs, implementing approved changes, and tracking performance data. A stakeholder raises concerns about the consistency and reliability of the outputs being produced. What should you do?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: B

Tại sao chọn B?

  • Đang execution — deliver outputs per plan & approved changes.
  • Concern về output consistency → verify against scope baseline requirements.
  • PMBOK: quality tại execution = deliverables theo agreed requirements.

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng
A. Reassess scope & quality plans Planning activity — chưa cần reopen plans; execution focus là conform to baseline.
C. Steering committee Escalate quá sớm — PM xử lý execution quality trước.
D. Full quality risk analysis Concern là output consistency vs requirements — không phải analyze risks mới.

Liên hệ Q27 Change Log

Q27: confusion về modifications → Change Log. Q28: output consistency → Scope baseline / execution per requirements.

5. Drill — Execution action nào?

Stakeholder doubts output matches agreed requirements
Approved scope fundamentally wrong — need replan
Team delivering outputs during execution
Issue beyond PM authority after analysis

0 / 4 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: “Consistency” → Reassess quality plans (A)

Execution = conform to baseline; reassess plans là overreaction khi chưa verify compliance.

Bẫy 2: Stakeholder concern → Escalate (C)

PM owns execution — đảm bảo deliverables per scope baseline trước khi escalate.

Pattern

Execution + output consistency/reliability → Scope baseline (B).

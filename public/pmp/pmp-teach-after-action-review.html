Bài học Q34 về After-Action Reviews (AAR) — sau khi hoàn thành critical migration phase, phát hiện discrepancies giữa expected và actual results, gây rework và missed timelines. PM nên làm gì next?

PMBOK Guide 8, p.24–26 & p.145 Manage Project Knowledge Câu 34 — Practice

1. After-Action Reviews (AAR)

AAR là structured process phân tích what happened, why it happened, và how it can be improved. Thường conducted sau projects hoặc major milestones để capture lessons learned và inform future work.

After-action reviews (PMBOK p.145)

A structured process used to analyze what happened, why it happened, and how it can be improved. Conducted after projects or major milestones to capture lessons learned and enhance team performance.

Đề: phase vừa completed + discrepancies discovered → đúng timing cho AAR trước khi tiếp tục phases tiếp theo.

2. AAR vs Risk Workshop vs SWOT

ToolMục đíchQ34 fit?
AAR (A) Reflect on completed work — what/why/how improve ✓ Phase completed, analyze discrepancies
Risk Workshop (B) Identify & assess future risks Backward-looking reflection, not risk identification
SWOT (C) Strategic analysis — Strengths/Weaknesses/Opportunities/Threats Org/strategy level — không phải milestone debrief
Adjust schedule (D) Recovery action on timeline/resources Symptom fix — chưa học từ discrepancies

Practice Questions PMBOK 8th Ed · Q34
You are managing a data migration project at VLM. After completing a critical migration phase, discrepancies are discovered between expected and actual results, leading to rework and missed timelines. What should you do next?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: A

Tại sao chọn A?

  • Major milestone completed + unexpected outcomes → AAR timing.
  • Analyze what/why/how improve — capture lessons before next phase.
  • Manage Project Knowledge tool (p.24–26, p.145).

Loại trừ từng đáp án

Đáp ánTại sao không đúng NEXT
B. Risk Workshop Forward-looking risk ID — đề cần reflect on completed phase outcomes.
C. SWOT analysis Strategic org analysis — không phải milestone after-action review.
D. Adjust schedule May follow AAR — nhưng chưa học why discrepancies occurred.

Liên hệ Q33 Lessons Learned

Q33: document vào lessons learned register. Q34: AAR là technique để generate lessons learned sau milestone — output đi vào register.

5. Drill — Tool nào?

Identify future threats before execution starts

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: Problems → Risk Workshop (B)

Risk workshop cho future risks — đề là reflect on completed phase với discrepancies.

Bẫy 2: Team issues → SWOT (C)

SWOT là strategic analysis — không phải structured milestone debrief (AAR).

Bẫy 3: Missed timelines → Adjust schedule (D)

Schedule fix không analyze root cause — AAR trước, recovery actions sau.

Pattern

After major milestone + unexpected outcomes → Conduct AAR (A).

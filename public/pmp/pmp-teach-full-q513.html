Answer

Solution gốc (CSV — cột P)

D. Use a fixed-budget method to disclose the available funds to the invited sellers and select the highest-ranking technical proposal within budget. The fixed-budget method allows sellers to adapt the scope and quality of their offer to the proposed budget. It is the best choice for when a project’s statement of work is precisely defined, no changes are anticipated, and the budget cannot be exceeded. The other options are incorrect. The least-cost method is appropriate for procurements of a standard or routine nature where well-established practices and standards exist. However, in more complex projects, the least-cost approach should be avoided because it may negatively impact quality. When using a quality-and-cost-based method, the buyer includes cost as a factor in the seller selection process as well as the quality of the technical solution offered, but quality should be considered a priority when compared to cost. It is not an ideal selection process for projects with a very limited budget. A qualifications-only method is a limited process in which the buyer creates a short list of approved vendors and selects the bidder with the strongest qualifications, references, and expertise. This approach is appropriate for projects where the value of procurement is relatively small; it would not apply to more complex projects.

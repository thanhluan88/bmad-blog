Practice Questions — PMBOK 8th Ed · Waterfall

Bài học Q106 về Precedence Diagramming Method (PDM) và dependencies FS / SS — Megan schedule renovation retail store tại Sideline Interiors. Flooring installation phải đợi painting hoàn thành, nhưng furniture assembly có thể bắt đầu khi painting đã bắt đầu. Sequence nào phản ánh đúng?

PMBOK Guide · PDM Schedule FS & SS Dependencies Câu 106 — Practice

1. Precedence Diagramming — FS & SS

PDM dùng nodes = activities, arrows = logical relationships. Four dependency types: FS (Finish-to-Start), SS (Start-to-Start), FF, SF. Default thường là FS.

FS and SS (A — đúng) Flooring waits until painting finished → FS (Paint → Floor). Furniture starts when painting started → SS (Paint → Furniture).
SS and SF (B — bẫy) SF rare — furniture không phải Start-to-Finish với painting.
FS and SF (C — bẫy) SF không khớp "begin once painting has started".
FF and FS (D — bẫy) FF = finish together — flooring phải wait painting finish (FS), không FF.

Signal Q106

Flooring must wait until painting fully completed → FS.
Furniture can begin once painting has started → SS.
Answer: A — FS and SS.

TypeMeaningQ106 mapping
FSSuccessor starts when predecessor finishesPainting → Flooring
SSSuccessor starts when predecessor startsPainting → Furniture assembly
FFFinish together✗ Not flooring scenario
SFSuccessor finishes when predecessor starts✗ Rare · not furniture

3. Câu hỏi thực hành — Question 106

Practice Questions · Waterfall · Q106
Megan is scheduling a retail store renovation at Sideline Interiors. Flooring installation must wait until painting is fully completed, but furniture assembly can begin once painting has started. Megan needs to define these relationships to avoid delays and rework. Which sequence best reflects this situation?

4. Phân tích đáp án — Đáp án đúng: A

  • FS: Painting (pred) → Flooring (succ) — floor waits until paint fully completed.
  • SS: Painting (pred) → Furniture (succ) — assembly begins when paint started.
  • PDM defines how predecessor and successor activities link in schedule network.

FS vs SS — Đọc stem chính xác

Wait until completed = FS. Begin once started = SS. Đừng nhầm với FF (finish together) hay SF (rare).

5. Drill — FS, SS, FF, hay SF?

Flooring cannot start until painting is fully done (Q106)
Furniture assembly begins when painting has started (Q106)
Code review cannot start until development is complete
Testing and documentation must finish at the same time
Site prep and utility marking can start together when project kicks off

0 / 5 đúng

6. Bẫy thi

Bẫy 1: "Started" → FS (wrong for furniture)

Begin once started = SS, không phải FS (wait until finished).

Bẫy 2: Two relationships → pick only FS

Stem có HAI relationships khác nhau — cần FS and SS (A).

Pattern

Wait until completed → FS. Begin once started → SS. Answer A.

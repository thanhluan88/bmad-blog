# 8 wrong patterns — exam cue → action

| Pattern | Cue | Do | Don't |
|---|---|---|---|
| Resilience | ambiguous impact on time/cost | anticipate · respond · recover | eliminate uncertainty / meeting-only |
| OPA actionable | lessons, estimation bias, vague policy | update OPA / templates / policy | archive-only / refer historical |
| Transparency | bad news, incomplete facts | share knowns + open dialog | distract · demand certainty |
| Engagement plan | resistant / high-influence SH | follow plan · adapt prefs + compliance | freestyle 1:1 · escalate-first |
| Coach conflict | “help me with teammate” | coach resolution techniques | PM takes over conflict |
| Compliance | local vs HQ ethics | legal validate + escalation | ethics exceptions |
| Value / verify | MVP / done / metrics | ship ready · verify scope · outcome+EV | wait perfect · vanity metrics |
| Money & contracts | budget, T&M, unpaid, SPI/CPI | forecast+act · MSA · admin-close · optimize+FT | pause · ignore close · overtime-only |

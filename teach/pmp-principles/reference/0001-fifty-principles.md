# 50 PMP exam mindset principles

Not the PMBOK 8 six principles — situational exam heuristics.

| # | Principle | VI cue |
|---|-----------|--------|
| 1 | Continuously identify and analyze stakeholders, not just at the beginning of the project. | Identify/analyze SH liên tục cả đời dự án — không chỉ lúc kickoff. |
| 2 | Actively engage all stakeholders, especially those who seem disengaged or unhappy. | Chủ động engage SH thờ ơ / không hài lòng — đừng bỏ qua. |
| 3 | Engage individual stakeholders where appropriate, rather than always involving the entire group. | Engage từng cá nhân khi cần — không bắt buộc họp cả nhóm mọi lúc. |
| 4 | Document all impacted individuals as stakeholders, even if their involvement is indirect. | Ai bị impact cũng vào register — kể cả gián tiếp. |
| 5 | Don't dismiss customer requests prematurely. Evaluate each one carefully. | Đừng bác request khách sớm — evaluate kỹ trước khi từ chối. |
| 6 | Traditional: Follow the plan. Do not allow changes without an approved change request. | Predictive: bám plan — không đổi nếu chưa có CR được duyệt. |
| 7 | Any stakeholder requesting changes to the project management plan must submit a change request. | Muốn đổi PMP → phải submit change request. |
| 8 | All changes must be reviewed and assessed. | Mọi change đều phải review/assess impact trước. |
| 9 | Traditional: Never act without a plan. Planning is done once for the entire project. | Predictive: không hành động không plan — plan upfront cả dự án. |
| 10 | Consult with your team and subject matter experts before making decisions, especially on technical issues. | Tham vấn team/SME trước khi quyết — đặc biệt kỹ thuật. |
| 11 | Choose actions that best serve project objectives and deliver the highest value to the stakeholders. | Chọn hành động tối ưu objective + value cho SH. |
| 12 | Address issues directly and avoid unnecessary escalation. | Xử lý issue trực tiếp — escalate chỉ khi cần. |
| 13 | Anticipate and address potential problems before they become critical issues. | Anticipate sớm — đừng đợi thành critical. |
| 14 | Always seek out the advice of the subject matter expert before moving forward on a project. Never assume you are the expert. | Hỏi SME trước khi tiến — đừng tự cho là expert. |
| 15 | Always investigate and consult before acting, especially when the questions ask what the PM should do first or next. | FIRST/NEXT → investigate/consult trước khi act. |
| 16 | Address conflict directly and privately. | Xung đột: xử lý trực tiếp + riêng tư. |
| 17 | Always protect the team from external disruptions and distractions. | Bảo vệ team khỏi nhiễu loạn bên ngoài. |
| 18 | Be a servant leader. | Servant leadership: phục vụ / remove impediments / empower. |
| 19 | Focus on the big picture, not just individual tasks or components. | Nhìn big picture — không chỉ task lẻ. |
| 20 | The team is best suited to break down work. | Team tự break down work tốt nhất. |
| 21 | Empower your team to make decisions and solve problems. | Empower team quyết định & solve problems. |
| 22 | Foster a collaborative environment where team members feel comfortable asking for help and offering support. | Môi trường cộng tác: hỏi giúp / hỗ trợ nhau thoải mái. |
| 23 | Ensure team members are cross-trained and have diverse skill sets. | Cross-train / đa kỹ năng để giảm bottleneck. |
| 24 | Provide timely and constructive feedback to your team members. | Feedback kịp thời + mang tính xây dựng. |
| 25 | Support and accommodate team requests for flexible work arrangements, if feasible. | Hỗ trợ linh hoạt giờ làm nếu khả thi. |
| 26 | Use peer programming for skill development. | Peer programming để phát triển kỹ năng. |
| 27 | Address performance issues directly and privately, not in front of the entire team. | Performance issue: riêng tư — không xử lý trước cả team. |
| 28 | The product owner documents and prioritizes features. Only the product owner prioritizes the product backlog. | Chỉ Product Owner ưu tiên product backlog. |
| 29 | Agile teams love collocation (being together in one central place to work). | Agile thích collocation khi có thể. |
| 30 | Face-to-face communication is the most effective way to communicate anything. | Face-to-face thường hiệu quả nhất (Agile Manifesto). |
| 31 | Gather customer feedback regularly and throughout the project life cycle. | Thu feedback khách thường xuyên suốt vòng đời. |
| 32 | Define quality requirements early and check them often. | Quality requirements sớm + kiểm thường xuyên. |
| 33 | Prioritize the highest value features in agile development. | Agile: ưu tiên feature giá trị cao nhất trước. |
| 34 | Focus on delivering working software frequently and iteratively. | Ship working software thường xuyên / iterative. |
| 35 | The team should provide estimates for their own work. | Team tự estimate việc của mình. |
| 36 | Actively identify and manage risks throughout the project. | Identify + manage risk chủ động suốt dự án. |
| 37 | Continuously identify and assess risk throughout the life cycle of your project. | Risk cadence liên tục — không one-shot đầu dự án. |
| 38 | Use mutually beneficial contracts and procurement. | Hợp đồng / procurement win–win. |
| 39 | Update the lesson learned register throughout the project. | Lessons learned cập nhật suốt dự án — không chỉ lúc đóng. |
| 40 | All projects should be formally closed, whether completed successfully, terminated, or terminated early, ensuring all bills are paid and resources released. | Mọi dự án đều formal close (kể cả dừng sớm): bills + release resources. |
| 41 | Repeat and reinforce the vision to the team. | Lặp lại / củng cố vision với team. |
| 42 | Clarify what success and failure look like. | Làm rõ success vs failure trông như thế nào. |
| 43 | In agile, the retrospectives are used to review and improve future sprints or iterations. | Retro: cải thiện sprint/iteration sau — không blame. |
| 44 | Implement feedback loops. Apply lessons from one task to the next. | Feedback loop: áp dụng lesson sang việc kế tiếp. |
| 45 | Avoid cost and time overruns. If you must choose, fix budget issues before scheduling. | Tránh overrun; nếu phải chọn — ưu tiên xử lý budget trước schedule (trong logic đề). |
| 46 | Traditional: Understand the critical path. | Predictive: nắm critical path. |
| 47 | Agile: Manage schedule through sprint commitments and velocity. | Agile schedule: sprint commitments + velocity. |
| 48 | When you see answers that are absolute, like "always" or "all," they're probably not correct. | Đáp án tuyệt đối (always/all) — thường sai. |
| 49 | Never do nothing. | Đừng chọn “không làm gì”. |
| 50 | The perfect answer isn't always listed. You must choose from the choices available. | Không có đáp án hoàn hảo — chọn best available. |
